How Orderflow came to be

For 18 years, Neurony Solutions has been building technology that solves real business problems. Across 800+ projects and 83+ clients, we kept seeing the same bottleneck in distribution: order intake.

Companies were growing, but their order desks weren't scaling. Teams were spending hours retyping emails, deciphering handwritten notes, and manually matching vague product descriptions to catalog entries. Every new customer or channel added more pressure on the same people.

We knew AI could do better. Not to replace the people who understand the business, but to handle the repetitive work that was holding them back. So we built Orderflow: a system that reads orders in any format, maps products to your catalog, and delivers clean, ERP-ready data without manual intervention.

What drives us

We automate complexity

Your customers send orders however is easiest for them. We handle the chaos so your team doesn't have to.

We keep humans in control

AI processes, humans verify. Low-confidence matches are flagged, not guessed. Your team stays in the loop without touching every order.

We learn from every order

Orderflow gets smarter the longer it runs. Product mapping precision increases over time, making the system more reliable with each order processed.

We integrate, not replace

Orderflow plugs into your existing workflow. Same email inbox, same ERP, same team. Just less manual work and fewer errors.
