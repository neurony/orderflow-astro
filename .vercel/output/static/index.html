Current limitations and operational friction

Every channel, every format, every day.

Email, WhatsApp, PDF, scan, photo, handwritten note. Your customer's order however is easiest for them. Your team absorbs the complexity every single time.

The knowledge lives in people, not systems.

Matching vague product descriptions to the right catalog entry is a skill built over years. It lives with specific staff members. When they're unavailable, orders wait.

You can't automate what isn't structured.

Traditional automation requires consistent, templated input. Your customers don't send templates, thus every increase in order volume means more manual work.

From inbox or WhatsApp to ERP without a single manual step.

Order Flow monitors your email and WhatsApp continuously, extracting all relevant information from messages, PDFs, scans, and handwritten notes the moment they arrive.

Every item is mapped to the right SKU automatically with a match score. Low-confidence matches are flagged for fast human review — your team stays in control without touching every order.

Every validated order exits as structured, ERP-ready data. Consistent format, every time, from every channel.

One system for every format your customers use.

Your customers won't change how they send orders. Let Orderflow handle whatever arrives in your inbox.

  • Free-text emails

    Unstructured body text with informal descriptions, missing codes and no fixed format.

  • Hand-written notes

    Scribbled lists, hand-filled order forms and warehouse notes with no standard structure.

  • WhatsApp images

    Shorthand requests, forwarded images and informal order threads sent via mobile.

  • Scanned documents

    Photographed or scanned paper order forms and PDF attachments.

  • Attached files

    Spreadsheets, PDFs and image files sent alongside or instead of email body text.

How Orderflow performs in production

Orderflow has been running in production at a high-volume distribution business. The results aren't projections or benchmarks. They're drawn from real order data, real catalog complexity, and real operational conditions.

  • ~98% of orders processed without any manual modification.

  • 80%+ of orders fully or partially product-mapped on first pass.

  • Accuracy improves over time. Product mapping precision steadily increases, making the system more reliable the longer it runs.

  • Significant and measurable. Reduction in manual intervention observed month over month after deployment.

  • Orders are pushed straight to the pipeline. Manual entry of orders from emails, whatsapp or handwritten notes is stripped away.

  • Volume spikes stop creating backlogs. The system processes at the same pace regardless of how many orders come in.

  • Every channel receives the same treatment. No order falls through because it came in from a different channel or in a different format.

  • Key staff dependency is reduced. The system learns to map vague descriptions to your catalog, ensuring order flow doesn't break.
